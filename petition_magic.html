Let me write out the story and case of my ticket.

I went to see my girlfriend and parked on a street corner. There were a couple of lots nearby that were vacant and there was overgrowth on parts of the sidewalk and in those lots.

That fact standing I found a parking, and took it. Later that night, I come to find that I was ticketed for parking within 10 feet of a hydrant. Unknowingly, I had parked next to a hydrant that was shrouded in the overgrowth of the brush that had hidden the hydrant verily behind it. I wanted to fight this ticket.

I saw that on my ticket, there was a court time of 1 pm on october 31st. I went into the facility, prepared to defend my case when I ended up standing in line with at least 7 other people here for the same reason. The ticket had reported court time, but that was apparently only the time that the ticket had to be paid by. The court date wasnt even issued, but on the back of the ticket, it states “...Please be advised that you must pleasd Not Guilty at least 7 days prior to the court date listed on your summons”.

I got mad and decided I should create a petition site that people can sign and submit their opinions en masse, and others can sign if they wanted things changed about forms and documents to avoid the hassle. Because why should we as people have to bear the burden of the government’s miscommunication to us?
